Lift Bro is a workout tracking app designed for lifters who want to track their progress and improve their strength.

Features:
- Track all your lifts with detailed set/rep/weight logging
- Monitor your progress over time with charts and analytics
- Calendar view to see your workout history
- Backup and restore your data
- Support for multiple exercise variations
- Track RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion) for each set
- Customizable settings to match your preferences

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced lifter, Lift Bro helps you stay consistent and track your gains.
